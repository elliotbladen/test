# 2026-05-06 - BetMate Positioning

## Core Direction

BetMate should be a 50/50 product:

- odds comparison for normal punters
- betting intelligence for smarter punters

The business is not just for advanced model users. It should work for everyone from a casual punter looking for the best price to a sharp user tracking CLV, model edge, line movement, and market disagreement.

Comparable broad positioning: SportPunter-style accessibility, but with stronger odds comparison, model intelligence, market movement, and CLV proof.

## Product Promise

The strongest positioning:

> Best odds plus betting intelligence.

The user journey should be:

> I came for best odds. I stayed because BetMate tells me which prices actually matter.

## Why The Hybrid Matters

Pure intelligence/model products are hard because users ask:

> Why should I trust your numbers?

Pure odds comparison products are hard because users ask:

> Why not just use an existing odds comparison site or bookmaker app?

The hybrid solves both:

1. Odds comparison gives instant utility and broad traffic.
2. Intelligence gives repeat use and trust.
3. CLV creates accountability.
4. Alerts create habit.
5. Affiliate/signup fees create early revenue.
6. SaaS/Plus creates later revenue.

## Audience

BetMate should serve:

- normal punters who just want the best available price
- casual users who want simple movement/edge indicators
- smarter bettors who care about line movement, CLV, model-vs-market, and pricing
- future Plus/SaaS users who want alerts, filters, history, exports, and deeper model breakdowns

Do not make the product feel like it is only for quants. Keep the odds comparison simple and immediate, then let users go deeper if they want.

## Product Structure

Recommended top-level product areas:

- Odds
  - fast odds comparison
  - true best price
  - bookmaker margin / overround
  - affiliate/signup links

- Movers
  - biggest price moves
  - biggest line moves
  - steam / drift
  - moved through key numbers where relevant

- Edges
  - model vs market
  - normal engine vs ML engine
  - matrix flags
  - injury/weather/news impact

- CLV
  - public proof
  - round-by-round
  - cumulative average
  - by sport/market/signal

- Tools
  - calculators
  - useful for broad SEO and casual users

- Alerts
  - future retention engine
  - model edge alerts
  - price movement alerts
  - injury/news alerts
  - saved watchlists

## Free vs Paid Direction

Free should include enough value to grow traffic:

- best odds
- basic movement
- selected model edges
- public CLV tracker
- basic daily dashboard

Paid/Plus later:

- custom alerts
- full model breakdown
- advanced filters
- matrix research
- saved watchlists
- CSV/export
- historical market movement
- deeper CLV history
- early access to ML/normal disagreements

## Trust Rules

If BetMate uses affiliate/signup revenue, trust must be protected.

Clear separation required:

- best price must be true best price
- sponsored placements must be labelled
- affiliate links cannot masquerade as model recommendations
- Betfair prices should account for commission
- CLV/results should remain public and honest

The goal is not to become another noisy tips site. The goal is to become the betting workflow product users check before they bet.
